Topple! – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2025

Topple! is a block-stacking arcade game developed by MacGyver. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Collect

Topple! does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not have servers, accounts, or analytics of any kind.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is saved only on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults:

  • Your high score and best stage reached
  • Whether you have completed the tutorial
  • Your chosen theme (Classic or Tron)
  • Whether you have purchased the Shapes Unlock

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Apple Game Center

Topple! uses Apple Game Center for leaderboards. If you are signed in to Game Center, your score may be submitted to the public leaderboard. This is handled entirely by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

In-App Purchases

Topple! offers one optional in-app purchase (Shapes Unlock). All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Topple! does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Topple! is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
